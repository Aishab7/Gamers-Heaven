LOL

League of Legends is a strategic multiplayer battle game where two teams of five compete to destroy the enemy’s Nexus. With diverse champions and regular updates, every match brings new challenges and tactics.

Champions

Lux

Role: Mage / Support.

Description: A radiant mage who controls light magic to deal damage and protect allies.

Fame: Known for her long-range Final Spark (R) and beginner-friendly gameplay.

Caitlyn

Role: Marksman.

Description: A skilled sniper and Piltover’s sheriff with the longest basic attack range among ADCs.

Fame: Strong in lane, great for controlling fights, and famous for her Ace in the Hole (R) ultimate.

Zed

Role: Assassin.

Description: A deadly ninja who uses shadows to strike and escape quickly.

Fame: Popular for his high burst damage, mobility, and outplay potential.
