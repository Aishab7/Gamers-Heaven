CS:GO

CS:GO is a tactical shooter where Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists compete in strategic missions. With competitive gameplay and global tournaments, it remains a top esports title.

Combat Teams

Gign (France):

Description: An elite French counter-terrorism unit specialized in hostage rescue and anti-terrorism operations.

Appearance: GIGN members wear dark blue uniforms with bulletproof vests and combat helmets.

Common Maps: Found on maps like Office and Bank.

Phoenix

Description: A militant Eastern European terrorist group involved in various criminal activities.

Appearance: They are characterized by brown leather jackets and red scarves, giving them a gang-like appearance.

Common Maps: They are frequently found on maps like Cache and Train.

SAS

Description: A renowned British special forces unit known for covert operations and counter-terrorism expertise.

Appearance: They wear all-black clothing with gas masks and heavy body armor.

Common Maps: Appear on maps like Inferno and Dust II.
