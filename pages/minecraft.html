Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players explore, build, and survive in a blocky world. It features Survival and Creative modes, offering endless possibilities. The game is known for its pixelated graphics and multiplayer experience.

Minecraft Characters

Steve

Description: Steve is one of the default player characters in Minecraft.

Appearance: Brown hair, blue shirt, dark pants.

Alex

Description: Alex is another default player character.

Appearance: Orange hair, green shirt, brown pants.

Creeper

Description: One of the most feared mobs in Minecraft.

Appearance: Green, four-legged creature with a frowning face.
